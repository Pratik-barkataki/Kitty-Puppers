About Us
“Kitty Puppers”

Kitty Puppers is a website intended to find a loving home for our fluffy friends where they can grow and thrive.

We shelter pets who are lost, wounded or is separated till we can find a new home for them. We urge you to adopt pets rather than shop them. Our little buddies may seem voiceless to us but they are never void of emotions. Let us make this world a better place for animals !

A little love is all they need !
